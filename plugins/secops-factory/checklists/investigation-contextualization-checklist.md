# Investigation Contextualization Checklist

**Weight:** 15%
**Purpose:** Verify business and operational context integration.

## Check Items

- [ ] Asset criticality assessed with justification
- [ ] Business impact evaluated (financial, operational, reputational)
- [ ] Affected systems/services and downstream dependencies identified
- [ ] Risk level combines alert severity with asset criticality and business impact
- [ ] Client/customer impact considered (if applicable)
- [ ] SLA/compliance implications noted (PCI-DSS, HIPAA, NERC CIP)
- [ ] Environmental factors documented (test vs prod, maintenance, scheduled changes)
