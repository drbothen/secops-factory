# Disposition Reasoning Checklist

**Weight:** 20%
**Purpose:** Verify logical, evidence-based disposition determination.

## Check Items

- [ ] Clear disposition stated (TP/FP/BTP)
- [ ] Reasoning supported by specific evidence (not just assertions)
- [ ] At least 2 alternative explanations considered and evaluated
- [ ] Confidence level stated (High/Medium/Low) with justification
- [ ] Escalation decision justified with criteria applied
- [ ] Business/operational context factored into decision
- [ ] Next actions clearly specified (tuning, escalation, monitoring, close)
